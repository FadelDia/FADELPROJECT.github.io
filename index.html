Visualization with Tableau

The dashboard created in Tableau provides a comprehensive view of earthquake data. It starts with a pie chart showing the proportion of earthquakes that have caused tsunamis. This is followed by a world map illustrating the global distribution of earthquake magnitudes, giving a geographical perspective of seismic activity.Next, a line chart shows the fluctuation of earthquake magnitudes in Peru and Russia over time, highlighting the seismic activity in these specific regions. A scatter plot then presents the relationship between the magnitude of earthquakes and their level of importance, providing insights into the severity and impact of these natural disasters.Finally, a histogram displays the distribution of earthquake magnitudes in 2022, offering a snapshot of seismic activity for that year.
